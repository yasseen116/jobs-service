We Are Hired

Bridging the gap between Egyptian talents and top employers.

Our Vision

We see a future where the job market in Egypt is completely transparent, efficient, and skill-driven.

Our goal is to become the #1 Digital Recruitment Infrastructure in the MENA region. We aim to replace outdated hiring methods with intelligent matching algorithms that empower every graduate to find their place and every company to build their dream team without the hassle.

  • ✔ Eliminate Application Fatigue
  • ✔ Standardize Hiring Processes
  • ✔ Bridge the Academic-Industry Gap

Our Mission

The recruitment market in Egypt is fragmented. Opportunities are scattered across social media, outdated websites, and closed networks.

Hired was born out of a simple idea: To create a centralized, intelligent platform where job seekers can find opportunities that actually match their needs (like Remote/Hybrid options) and employers can reach the right candidates instantly.
